Population Growth is Slowing Down, Where Is the Problem?

As the world has just recently reached 8 billion population, the population growth has been slower than what many experts predicted years ago. This happens due to many countries started to have much slower birth rate than expected.

Global Birth Rate per Female

To maintain a stable population, each female needs to have an average birth rate of 2. A birth rate lower than 2 would cause poulation decrease and birth rate higher than 2 would increase population.

How does economy affect birth rate?

Countries with higher gdp per capita tends to have lower birth rate, this might happens due to a higher living cost to raise a child in these countries. While countries with lower gdp tends to have a higher birth rate.

Where is the problem?

Looking at Korea as an example. Korea has one of the lowest birth rate in the world. Looking at its age group pyramid, we could see that from 1990 to 2020, the weight have shifted up. meaning the median age in Korea has significantly gets older. As the birth rate get lowers, it means there are lesser young people to replace the old one.
